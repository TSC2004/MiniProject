🍰 Chocolate Cake

Prep Time: 45 minutes

Serves: 4

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1.5 cups sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 0.5 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix all dry ingredients.
  3. Add eggs and milk, then mix well.
  4. Pour into a greased pan.
  5. Bake for 30–35 minutes.
